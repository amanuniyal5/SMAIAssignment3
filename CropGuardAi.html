The Context
The Problem
Indian agriculture loses crores every year to misdiagnosed crop disease. A farmer photographs a sick leaf with their phone, but farmers lack instant expert-level diagnostic tools in the field, relying on guesswork or delayed consultations that cost critical treatment windows.
The Solution & Work Done
We built a unified Streamlit web app and PWA for 14 crops. By fine-tuning a MobileNetV3-Small architecture for 3 epochs on the PlantVillage dataset, it predicts among 38 disease classes. It provides a confidence bar, a farmer-friendly explanation (JSON cache), and actionable next steps.

Action Plan
Core App Features
Offline-ready PWA + TFLite
On-device inference via MobileNetV3-Small → TFLite. No internet needed in the field.
XAI Interpretability Suite
GradCAM, SHAP, LIME & Occlusion Maps show farmers why the AI flagged a leaf region.
Treatment Cost Estimator
Chemical recommendations with ₹-per-acre cost + Open-Meteo weather-risk correlation.
SQLite Field Diary
Tracks infection progression over time. WhatsApp report card for agronomist sharing.
Ablation Study Dashboard
5-architecture comparison on accuracy vs. latency with experiment SQLite tracker.
Docker Multi-Service
docker-compose bundles main app, ablation dashboard, and experiment tracker together.
Real-World Impact
Why it matters: Indian farmers face massive crop losses due to difficult-to-diagnose diseases visually similar to the naked eye. By packaging an accurate MobileNetV3 model in an offline-ready PWA with 11.4ms edge latency, we bring expert-level tools to the field. Actionable, instant, and data-driven crop management is now in the pocket of any farmer with a smartphone!
